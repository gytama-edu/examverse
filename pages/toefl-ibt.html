Updated TOEFL iBT 2026 overview

Practice the skill—and the specific task.

The updated TOEFL iBT focuses on real communication, academic readiness, sentence control, listening accuracy, speaking fluency, and clear writing.

Students should practice by task type, not only by general skill. Knowing the goal and steps of each task makes practice more focused and useful.

i Exam Verse focuses on learning and practice. It does not save student progress.

Understand your result

TOEFL iBT 2026 Score Guide

The new scale gives students a simpler view of performance across all four skills.

From January 21, 2026, TOEFL iBT uses a 1–6 score scale. Scores may use half-point increments.

Students receive a score for Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking. The overall score is based on those four section scores.

During the transition period, a comparable 0–120 score may also appear. Use scores to find your next skill target—not as a reason to memorize answers.

1–2Building foundation
3–3.5Developing user
4–4.5Strong intermediate to upper-intermediate
5–5.5Advanced academic user
6Highly advanced user
Smart study strategy

How to Study TOEFL iBT 2026 Smarter

Small, deliberate habits make practice easier to repeat and improve.

01

Practice by task type

Learn a clear method for each task instead of treating every question the same.

02

Review mistakes with explanations

Ask why an answer worked and what clue you missed.

03

Build vocabulary from context

Keep useful words with the sentence or situation where you found them.

04

Train listening with short notes

Record only key ideas, changes, examples, and important details.

05

Speak clearly before speaking perfectly

Prioritize an understandable message, then refine accuracy and delivery.

06

Write with purpose, tone, and structure

Know your reader, your goal, and the order of your ideas before writing.
