How it works

One task at a time, one navigator for all twenty items, and a full review after submission.

  1. 01Press Start Mini Mock when you are ready.
  2. 02Complete the 10 inline word gaps in the first task.
  3. 03Answer the five daily-life questions and the five academic questions.
  4. 04Use the 1 to 20 navigator to jump to any item.
  5. 05Submit when finished and review all three sections.
