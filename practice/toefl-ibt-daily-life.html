Task logic guide

Read the text like a real message, notice the purpose, then choose the best answer.

  1. 01Read the whole text first.
  2. 02Answer the main idea or purpose question.
  3. 03Check a specific detail next.
  4. 04Use inference or vocabulary clues for the last question.
  5. 05Review the evidence before moving on.
